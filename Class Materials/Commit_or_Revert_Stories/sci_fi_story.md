# Mission to the Unknown

## Launch
The crew prepared for a journey unlike any other, bound for a distant planet. This planet is large, and known to be covered in festering swamps with limited elevation seen across the planets entire surface. This place is haunting, with previous mission crews coming back changed people losing themselves in the vastness of it all.
